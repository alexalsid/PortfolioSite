Hi! I’m Alex Alsid. Developer, designer, and recent Philadelphia transplant. I currently trudging through my sophomore year at Drexel University, studying Interactive Digital Media and falling in love with my new city. While at this point in my career my list of achievements are small, my list of ambitions certainly isn’t. Before coming to college I completely fell in love with the world of digital media, and what started as a podcast fueled hobby has become the center of my life. My passion is, and has always been digital storytelling. For the majority of my young life I believed I wanted to be a journalist, and it wasn’t until I took my first design class in high school that I realized there was a realm of storytelling that was being entirely under utilized. The concept of digital media as a tool to communicate our stories has since thrilled and motivated me with the shear endlessness of possibilities.
